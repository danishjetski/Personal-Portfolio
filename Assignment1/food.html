Favourite Food

NASI LEMAK

Nasi Lemak is a traditional Malaysian dish consisting of rice cooked in coconut milk, sambal,chicken,nuts,anchovies and cucumber. Nasi Lemak is also known to many as Malaysia's nasional dish.

MAGGI GORENG

Maggi Goreng is a simple dish that everybody can cook as all you need are the noodles,some oil and your good to go. Maggi Goreng can be eaten with anything such as eggs,chicken,beef and many more.

SUSHI

Sushi, is a Japanese dish that consists of many different ingredients but the basic base of a sushi is rice and seaweed. After that,any ingredients can be added such as crabsticks,eggs and chicken.

MCDONALD'S GCB

GCB,also known as Grilled Chicken Burger is a burger that consists of boneless chicken thigh with black pepper,lettuce and mayonnaise. A great thing about this meal is you can have a side and a drink if you want to.
